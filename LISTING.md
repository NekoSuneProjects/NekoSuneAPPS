## Microsoft Store

**Short description (≤100 chars):**

**Long description:**
> NekoSuneAPPS is a polished VRChat OSC companion that pushes your live data
> straight into the in-game chatbox and onto your avatar.
>
> • Chatbox + auto-rotating status presets with tokens
> • AudioLink spectrum (Low/Bass/Mid/Treble) over OSC
> • Now Playing (Windows media) with KAT support
> • Heart rate via Pulsoid & HypeRate
> • Component & network stats, active-window activity
> • TikTok / Twitch / Kick follower counters
> • IntelliChat AI rewrite/translate
> • Discord Rich Presence — shows your VRChat world with a one-click Join button
> • Auto-AFK announcements, stopwatch, calculator
> • OBS now-playing overlay
>
> Themeable UI, low resource usage, runs in the tray. Windows, macOS & Linux.
